          - "**Compatibility with Project Requirements**: Assess fit within overall project requirements."

          Utilize the following CSI sections applicable to product reviews:
          - Division 00: Procurement and Contracting Requirements
          - Division 01: General Requirements
          - Specific divisions related to product types (e.g., 03 for Concrete, 06 for Wood and Plastics).
